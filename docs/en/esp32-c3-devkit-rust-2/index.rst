=======================
ESP32-C3-DevKit-RUST-2
=======================

:link_to_translation:`zh_CN:[中文]`

ESP32-C3-DevKit-RUST-2 is a development board based on `ESP32-C3-MINI-1 <https://documentation.espressif.com/esp32-c3-mini-1_datasheet_en.html>`_, a general-purpose module with 4 MB SPI flash. This board integrates complete Wi-Fi and Bluetooth® Low Energy functions.
