ESP32-S31-Korvo-1
=================

:link_to_translation:`zh_CN:[中文]`

The ESP32-S31-Korvo-1 is a multimedia development board based on the ESP32-S31 chip with the ESP32-S31-WROOM-3 module. It includes a dual-microphone array for speech recognition and near/far-field wake-up; integrates peripherals such as LCD, camera, and microSD; supports JPEG-based video streaming; and targets low-cost, low-power, connected audio/video and graphical UI product development. This document describes revision V1.1.
